# Long Building — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 22 May 2026

## Summary

**Long Building does not collect or share any personal data of its own.** The
core game runs fully offline and stores all progress on your device.

The app includes Google AdMob advertising — rewarded videos (you choose
whether to watch) and short interstitial breaks between games. To serve
ads, AdMob collects device identifiers and usage data under its own
privacy policy, and on iOS may use these for tracking — see *Advertising*
below.

## What information we store on your device

The app stores the following only on your device via `UserDefaults`:

- Highest score, highest tower (block count)
- Total games played, total perfect placements
- Earned coins, unlocked block styles
- Preferences (music, sound effects, haptic intensity)

This data never leaves your device, has no cloud sync, and is wiped if
you uninstall the app.

## What we do NOT do

- The game itself does not collect your name, email, contacts, location,
  photos, or any other personal information.
- The game itself runs no analytics. (Google AdMob, used for ads,
  collects device identifiers and usage data for advertising and
  measurement — see *Advertising* below.)
- We do not require an account.
- The core gameplay does not require an internet connection.

## Advertising

The app uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob):

- AdMob collects device identifiers (Advertising Identifier / IDFA) and
  usage data **to serve ads**, and may use them for tracking across apps
  and websites owned by other companies.
- AdMob's own privacy policy applies:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
- On iOS, the app requests permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
  before any tracking begins. If you decline, AdMob receives a
  zeroed/limited identifier and serves non-personalised ads.
- Two kinds of ads may appear:
  - **Rewarded video**, only when you tap a button asking for it
    (in exchange for an in-game coin bonus).
  - **Interstitial**, between roughly every fifth game. You can avoid
    them entirely by not playing many games in a row.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). The game itself collects
nothing. AdMob is configured to follow Apple's age-appropriate ad rules
and disable personalised ads for users in regulated regions.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data handling changes, this document will be updated and the
version bumped in the in-app About screen.
